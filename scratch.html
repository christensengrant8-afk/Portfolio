The Thrill of Snowboarding

My Passion for Powder and Progression

Why I Love Snowboarding

Snowboarding isn't just a sport—it's a lifestyle, an addiction, and a form of pure freedom. There's nothing quite like carving down a mountain with fresh powder under your board.

Whether it's the adrenaline rush of hitting a big jump, the satisfaction of landing a new trick, or simply the peaceful glide through untouched snow—snowboarding offers something unique every time you strap in.

Essential Tricks to Master

Progression Path for Beginners to Advanced:

  1. Beginner Level
    • Ollie - The foundation of all tricks
    • 180s - Half rotation spins
    • Basic grabs - Method, Indy, Mute
    • Straight airs - Getting comfortable in the air
  2. Intermediate Level
    • 360s - Full rotation spins
    • Backside and frontside boardslides
    • 50-50 grinds on boxes and rails
    • Switch riding - Riding opposite stance
  3. Advanced Level
    • 540s and 720s - Multiple rotations
    • Backflips and frontflips
    • Butter tricks - Presses and spins on snow
    • Technical rail combinations
  4. Expert Level
    • Double corks - Double off-axis flips
    • Triple corks - The holy grail
    • Complex rail transfers
    • Backcountry big mountain lines
